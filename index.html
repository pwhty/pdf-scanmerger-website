Smart interleaving

Pages are automatically interleaved in the correct order. Optionally reverse even pages if needed.

Automatic pairing

Drop two files at once — the app detects which file belongs where based on the filename.

Three steps to a finished document

1

Scan

Scan all front pages first, then all back pages of your document.

2

Load

Drop both PDF files into the app — or let batch mode handle it automatically.

3

Merge

Click "Merge" — and your correctly ordered document is ready.
